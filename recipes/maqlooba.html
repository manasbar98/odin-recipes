Maqlooba Recipe

Description

Maqlooba is a traditional Palestinian dish served throughout the Levant. It consists of meat, rice, and fried vegetables placed in a pot which is flipped upside down when served, hence the name maqlooba, which translates literally as "upside-down."

Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice
  • Chicken
  • Tomato Paste
  • Spices
  • Tomato
  • Potato
  • Eggplant
  • Onion
  • Pepper
  • Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. Prepare eggplant and potato by slicing and drizzling oil and salt, then roast at 450F for 30 minutes
  2. Add spices to chicken and fry until edges are golden brown
  3. Slice onions and peppers before cooking on stovetop with salt and oil until soft
  4. Layer vegetables in bottom of the pot, followed by chicken and then rice
  5. Add water or chicken stock followed by spices and tomato paste
  6. Cook on medium high heat until water bubbles, then cover and reduce heat to low and cook for 50 minutes
  7. Turn off heat and allow pot to rest for a few minutes before serving

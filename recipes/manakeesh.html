Manakeesh Recipe

Description

Manakeesh are a Middle-eastern flatbread commonly topped with za'aatar or a blend of cheeses. Manakeesh are typically eat for breakfast and are best paired with a glass of mint tea.

Ingredients

  • Middle Eastern Bread dough
  • Blend of Cheeses: Akawi and Mozzarella
  • Sesame seeds
  • Za'atar
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cut dough into equal pizza-sized pieces
  2. Mix olive oil and Za'atar and spread over flatbread
  3. Sprinkle cheese over top
  4. Bake for 5 - 7 minutes

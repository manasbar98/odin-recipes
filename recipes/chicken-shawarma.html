Chicken Shawarma Recipe

Description

Shawarma is a popular dish originating from the Middle East. It's a popular street food that is traditionally made in the form of spiced meats on a stacked vertifical rotisserie. Shawarma is commonly eaten in sandwich form as a quick lunch or dinner or even a snack between meals, though it can be the center piece of a healthy bowl as well.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon
  • Garlic
  • Spice blend: coriander, cumin, cardamom, paprika, cayenne, salt, and pepper
  • Yogurt
  • Pita bread
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Marinade chicken with yogurt, lemon, spices, garlic, and olive oil in fridge overnight
  2. Make yogurt sauce by mixing yogurt, lemon, cumin, garlic, salt, and pepper
  3. Cook chicken on stove top until charred
  4. Rest chicken for 3 minutes
  5. Assemble wrap with yogurt sauce, lettuce, and tomatoes
